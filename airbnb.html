    Brief

    In a mobile app, provide a way to combine photos and/or notes and transform their shared family trips into immersive stories about the culture and destinations they visit. What if that solution combined a traditional pledge with a fun new way to spread the word, visually, through social media?

    Visual Design Ideation

    Presenting Journals


    Group travelers come in all shapes and sizes. From families with young ones they want to protect, to students headed off on their first big conference. Airbnb has been there to provide the best lodging and experiences for travelers.


    Sharing those memories with the ones we love has taken many travelers to far away apps. Photos once freshly posted decay in a series of long group chats and families who want a secure and safe way to save and share their travel photos have to resort to unwieldy and user hostile solutions to just keep g-ma in the loop.

    Journals is a unified portal to keep your trips organized by listing and curating events that your group has booked to keep everyone on the same page.


    Group members can post photos, stills or ephemeral to share memories to close friends and family. At the end of the trip, journals will help you curate and share those memories to even those who weren’t physically with you but spiritually there.

    An itinerary and scrapbook in your pocket


    After your trips, you can even share stories with your hosts to let them know about how great your experiences were. And with your permission, you can securely have your stories on listing pages. Journals is the number one way our Airbnb community is able to connect with each other while we are in the sea, sky or land.

    Closing



    After a long 48 hours of non-stop designing, we are humbly proud to have placed 2nd place for the semi-finalist round out of 1500 creative and ambitious entries. We were fairly new to Adobe XD, so having to explore the capabilities of this prototyping software was very insightful to the implementation of our collective vision for Airbnb Journals. We were judged by an Airbnb creative director and experience director through an online livestream.


    Moving forward, we would like to validate the user flows and prototype through user testing, and find the most efficient entry points to ensure a cohesive and adoptive user journey.
